ABOUT NANAKI CREATIONS

Discover the story behind our handcrafted treasures

Our Story

NANAKI CREATIONS was born from a passion for preserving traditional handicrafts while bringing a modern touch to timeless designs. Founded by Nanaki, a skilled artisan with over a decade of experience, our brand represents the perfect blend of heritage and contemporary aesthetics.

We specialize in creating beautiful home décor items including quilts, cushion covers, pouches, table runners, and festive buntings. Every piece we create tells a story of dedication, creativity, and love for the craft. From intricate quilting patterns to delicate embroidery work, each item is meticulously handcrafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Our mission is to celebrate the beauty of handmade artistry while providing sustainable livelihoods to local artisans. We believe that in a world of mass production, there's something truly special about owning a piece that has been lovingly crafted by hand.
